EMPOWER DS
DELIVERY SYSTEM

SPECIFICATIONS

Application-Specific Treatment Attachments: 4
Includes: Patented Laser-Contact Ball
Fiber: Premium, Double-sheathed, and Rubber Coated
Handpiece: Finger Switch

Empower DS provides the versatility you need to treat your toughest cases. Four application-
specific treatment heads, the patented laser-contact ball and the toughest fiber available make
Pegasus therapy lasers clinically practical and ruggedly durable. A heavy duty, extended-length
fiber allows you to safely and easily treat patients without moving the system. Pegasus's
superior Platinum warranty keeps your investment safe for 36 months. The comprehensive,
"bumper-to-bumper" warranty includes a loaner provision and covers the entire system
including the treatment heads, handpieces, and fiber.
